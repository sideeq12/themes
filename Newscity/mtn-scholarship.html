MTN Scholarships 2020

The annual scholarship scheme organized by the Communication company for the year 2020 application is now available , this scholarship application is categorised in to two, base on the physical functionalities , which are :

  • MTN SCIENCE AND TECTHNOLOGY SCHEMES
  • MTN SCHOLARSHIP FOR BLIND STUDENTS

In order to be Eligible to apply for the scholarship, the candidate MUST be a 200 level student and above and this clearly shows that the recently admitted students to various institution will have to firstly have a cummulative GPA as this is part of the requirements.
All applicant must have a verified email, School ID card and Guardian phone , coupled with a passport which will be used during the application


There are only three centers for the exam which are located in LAGOS, ABUJA and OWERRI respectively. The approved applicants will be contacted through either the email, phone number or any other kind of communcation method submited when the scholarshipis being applied. kindly take note of the credentials when applying for the scholarship. click HERE to apply.
